List of vintage or obsolete Boiby products

This page provides information about Boiby products that are classified as Vintage or Obsolete.

About vintage products

Vintage products are those that have not been sold for more than 6 years. Limited service and parts may still be available through Boiby or authorized providers.

About obsolete products

Obsolete products are those that have not been sold for more than 10 years. These products no longer receive support from Boiby and finding repair parts will be difficult, but customer service will attempt to help you where possible.
