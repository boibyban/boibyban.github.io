List of vintage, obsolete, or historical Boiby products

This page provides information about Boiby products that are classified as Vintage, Obsolete, or Historical.

About vintage products

Vintage products are those that have not been sold for more than 5 years. Limited service and parts may still be available through Boiby or authorized providers.

About obsolete products

Obsolete products are those that have not been sold for more than 8 years. These products no longer receive support from Boiby and finding repair parts will be difficult.

About historical products

Historical products are those that have not been sold for more than 12 years. Boiby generally does not repair these, though in rare cases Boiby may help end users find parts. No replacement parts are stocked.
