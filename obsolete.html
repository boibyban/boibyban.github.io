List of Vintage, Obsolete, or Historical Boiby Products

This list outlines Boiby products that are no longer sold and categorised by their age since discontinuation. Vintage products are not sold for 5 years, obsolete for 8 years, and historical for 12 years.

About vintage products

Vintage products are those that have not been sold for at least 5 years but less than 8 years. Boiby may still offer limited service for these products, and parts may still be available but are becoming harder to source.

About obsolete products

Obsolete products are those that have not been sold for 8 years or more. These products no longer receive support from Boiby, and it will be harder to find repair parts or source them from official channels.

About historical products

Historical products are those not sold for 12 years or more. Unless it is a special case, Boiby will not repair these devices. Boiby may help end users find parts, but there is only limited support and Boiby does not stock any replacement parts for these products.
